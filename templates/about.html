About EchoAid

EchoAid is an intelligent voice-based assistant designed to empower individuals who are visually impaired or blind. Using cutting-edge AI technologies like speech recognition, text-to-speech, OCR, and computer vision, EchoAid offers real-time voice interaction, object detection, scene description, and navigation guidance.

Built with compassion and innovation, EchoAid is your personal AI companion that speaks, listens, and guides you through daily life.
